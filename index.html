Meet Pete

A lifetime of flying, learning, and living deliberately

The LIFE Briefing is created by Pete Schuil — a Zimbabwean Chartered Accountant, commercial pilot, author and mentor with more than four decades of professional experience across aviation and business advisory work in Southern Africa.

After a long career flying charter routes and helping companies across the region build, fund and restructure their operations, Pete now focuses on personal development and health coaching. He shares what that lifetime of preparation, discipline and lived experience taught him through a short daily audio briefing delivered to subscribers on WhatsApp.

The LIFE Briefing — Living Intentionally for Excellence — offers practical, grounded insight on mindset, lifestyle and personal growth in about five minutes a day. To subscribe, simply message JOIN.
